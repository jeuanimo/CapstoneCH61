================================================================================
CHATBOT Q&A ENTRY GUIDELINES
================================================================================

WHAT TO INCLUDE (Public Information Only):
-------------------------------------------
✓ Fraternity history and traditions
✓ Mission and values
✓ General membership information
✓ Event dates and descriptions
✓ Program information
✓ Leadership team names/titles
✓ Contact information
✓ Chapter location and meeting times
✓ Public achievements and records
✓ Community service initiatives
✓ General FAQs

WHAT TO NEVER INCLUDE (Sensitive Information):
-----------------------------------------------
✗ Passwords or login credentials
✗ Private member information
✗ Financial data or dues amounts
✗ Security procedures
✗ Personal phone numbers or addresses
✗ Private email addresses
✗ Medical or health information
✗ Internal member disputes
✗ Confidential committee discussions
✗ Social media credentials
✗ Probation or disciplinary records
✗ Member-only content
✗ Internal ritual information

================================================================================
WRITING EFFECTIVE Q&A ENTRIES
================================================================================

QUESTION FIELD (50-500 characters):
- Be clear and specific
- Write as a natural question someone would ask
- Examples:
  ✓ "What is the mission of Phi Beta Sigma?"
  ✓ "When are chapter meetings held?"
  ✓ "How do I get involved in community service?"

KEYWORDS FIELD (comma-separated, lowercase):
- Include variations of the question
- Think about synonyms
- Include single-word terms
- Examples:
  ✓ "mission, values, purpose, fraternity goals"
  ✓ "meetings, chapter meetings, when, schedule, time"
  ✓ "community service, service, volunteer, involvement"

ANSWER FIELD (50+ characters):
- Be clear and helpful
- Keep sentences simple
- Provide specific details
- 150-300 words is ideal
- Add a call-to-action if appropriate
- Example:

  "Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc. was founded on January 9, 1914,
   at Howard University in Washington, D.C. Our fraternity is built
   on three pillars: Culture for Service, Service for Humanity, and
   Brotherhood. The Nu Gamma Sigma Chapter exemplifies these values
   through regular community service projects, academic support
   programs, and social events that strengthen our chapter and
   surrounding community.
   
   For more information, visit our website or contact a chapter officer."

================================================================================
CONFIDENCE THRESHOLD GUIDELINES
================================================================================

Lower Thresholds (20-35):
- For broad, frequently asked questions
- For questions with multiple keyword variations
- For common synonyms
- Use when casting a wider net
- Example: "What is a fraternity?" threshold: 25

Medium Thresholds (35-50):
- For moderately specific questions
- Default threshold: 30
- For most standard Q&A entries
- Example: "What are our meeting times?" threshold: 40

Higher Thresholds (50-100):
- For very specific questions
- When you want exact matches
- For technical or detailed topics
- Example: "Who is the current chapter president?" threshold: 70

================================================================================
CATEGORY SELECTION
================================================================================

About the Fraternity:
- History, mission, values, general info
- Examples: "Founded in 1914...", "Our mission is..."

History & Traditions:
- Founding story, traditions, symbols
- Examples: "Our colors are...", "Stroll means..."

Membership:
- How to join, membership requirements
- Examples: "How do I become a member?", "Requirements"

Events & Programs:
- Chapter events, programs, activities
- Examples: "Events calendar", "Community service"

Contact Information:
- How to reach the chapter, emails, locations
- Examples: "Where do you meet?", "How to contact us"

Values & Mission:
- Core values, mission statement, purpose
- Examples: "What do you stand for?", "Our values"

Frequently Asked Questions:
- Common visitor questions
- Examples: "What's the difference...", "When...", "Why..."

Other:
- Anything that doesn't fit above categories

================================================================================
TESTING YOUR ENTRIES
================================================================================

After adding an entry, test the chatbot:

1. Go to http://localhost:8000/chatbot/
2. Try exact question: "What is the mission of Phi Beta Sigma?"
3. Try synonym: "Who founded the fraternity?"
4. Try partial match: "mission"
5. Try keyword: "values"
6. Verify answer is returned correctly

If not working:
- Check keywords include all search terms
- Lower confidence threshold
- Verify entry is marked "Active"
- Check for typos in keywords

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING ENTRY ISSUES
================================================================================

Entry not appearing in search:

1. Check "Is Active" is checked
2. Verify keywords are lowercase
3. Check confidence threshold isn't too high
4. Ensure keywords match search terms
5. Test with exact question first

Entry appears for wrong queries:

1. Check keywords aren't too broad
2. Raise confidence threshold
3. Make keywords more specific
4. Remove ambiguous terms

User getting "no match" responses:

1. Check database has active entries
2. Add more diverse keywords
3. Add more Q&A entries
4. Lower confidence thresholds on popular topics
