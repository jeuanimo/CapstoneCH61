How to Set Up Twilio
  1. Create Twilio Account

    Go to twilio.com and sign up for an account

  2. Get Your Credentials

    In your Twilio Console (Project Settings), copy your:

    • Account SID
    • Auth Token
  3. Get Your Phone Number

    Purchase or claim a Twilio phone number from the phone numbers section

  4. Enter Credentials Here

    Paste your credentials above and save

  5. Test Mode First

    Start with Test Mode enabled to verify everything works before sending real SMS

  6. Enable Live Mode

    Once verified, uncheck Test Mode and check Enable SMS Alerts to go live
