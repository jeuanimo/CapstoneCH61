Program Overview

The Scholarship & Education program is a cornerstone of Nu Gamma Sigma Chapter's commitment to academic excellence. We believe that education is the key to unlocking individual potential and creating positive change in our communities.

Key Initiatives

Academic Mentorship

One-on-one and group mentoring sessions connecting members with students seeking academic guidance. We provide tutoring, study skills development, and college preparation support.

Scholarship Awareness

Annual scholarship workshops and information sessions helping students identify and apply for educational funding opportunities. We also administer our own chapter scholarship program.

Educational Workshops

Workshops covering topics such as financial literacy, career readiness, resume building, and professional development to prepare students for success beyond the classroom.

Youth Programs

Partnerships with local schools to provide academic support, motivational speaking, and positive role modeling for K-12 students in our community.
